NIST-Aligned Risk Assessment Tool

Risk assessment helps an organization identify what could go wrong, estimate how likely it is, understand how serious the consequences could be, and decide what to do next. This no-backend tool uses a simple risk register format inspired by the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0 and NIST SP 800-30 concepts such as threat events, control gaps, likelihood, impact, and treatment.

Framework basis: Use NIST CSF 2.0 for the on-screen function categories and a simple Likelihood × Impact scoring model for prioritization.

How this calculator works

Follow the steps below to create a quick, structured cybersecurity risk assessment:

  1. Enter a short risk statement and optional notes for each row.
  2. Select the NIST CSF function that best fits the risk: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, or Recover.
  3. Optionally describe the threat event and control gap to make the risk easier to understand later.
  4. Assign a Likelihood score from 1 to 5 and an Impact score from 1 to 5.
  5. The tool calculates an Overall Risk Score by multiplying likelihood and impact.
  6. Choose a treatment approach such as Mitigate, Transfer, Accept, or Avoid.
  7. Use Download CSV or Download PDF to save your results before leaving the page.

Note: All data entered here is temporary and stays in the browser only for the current page session. Refreshing or leaving the page resets the entries unless you export them first.
